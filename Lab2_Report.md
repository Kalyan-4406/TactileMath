## Product Description
### About the Use Case
**Why this portal is required for autism kids?**
Children with autism often face challenges with traditional learning methods due to difficulties in processing abstract concepts and maintaining focus. A dedicated portal provides:
- **Visual Learning**: Using images and interactive elements to explain math concepts.
- **Engagement**: Gamified elements to keep the child interested.
- **Customized Pacing**: Allowing the child to learn at their own speed.

**Challenges in autism kids addressed:**
- **Attention Deficit**: Interactive games reduce distraction.
- **Abstract Thinking**: Visual aids make abstract math concrete.
- **Hypersensitivity**: Soothing colors and simple UI avoid sensory overload.

**Highlights and Novelty:**
- **Adaptive Difficulty**: The games adjust based on the child's progress.
- **Visual First Approach**: Every concept is paired with a visual cue.
- **Input Variety**: Supports mouse and keyboard interactions.

**Importance of Visualisation:**
Visual inputs are often processed faster and more effectively by autistic children. This portal leverages this strength by converting numbers into objects (e.g., counting apples instead of just the number '5'), making learning intuitive and less stressful.

## Improvements for Autism Kids
1.  **Memory Improvement**: Repeating patterns helps strengthen working memory.
2.  **Contextual Learning**: Associating numbers with real-world objects.
3.  **Motor Skills**: Clicking and dragging improves hand-eye coordination.

## Algorithms Implemented
1.  **Random Number Generation**: For creating infinite variations of math problems.
2.  **Pattern Matching**: To validate the user's input against the expected sequence.
3.  **Score Tracking**: simple counter to provide positive reinforcement.

## Future Enhancements
1.  **AI Adaptation**: Using ML to analyze response times and adjust difficulty dynamically. *Required to tailor the experience to each child's unique needs.*
2.  **Voice Interaction**: Adding speech-to-text for non-verbal children. *Required to make the app accessible to a wider range of users.*
3.  **Parent Dashboard**: detailed analytics for parents/teachers. *Required to track long-term progress.*
